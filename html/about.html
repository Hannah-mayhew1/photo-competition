About the Picture Perfect photography competition:

The Picture Perfect photography competition started out in 2004 with thousands of entries pouring in every year since. The purpose of this competition is to embrace the art of photography and share individual talent across the globe. The competition is open to all nationalities allowing us to progressively capture every corner of the world.

Hannah Mayhew - Founder of the Picture Perfect Photography competition
